Profile

Looking at my results from the data, I am a visual learner and if you look at both the Myers
-Briggs Test and Big Five Personality Test it is easy to see a definite similarity in the
areas of introversion, Neuroticism, and Agreeableness/Feeling. I think it is plausible to
assume that the data points that are consistent between the two different personality
tests are most likely true whereas the outliers; judging/conscientiousness, imagination,
and intuition can mostly be ignored or taken with a grain of salt.


I am an “Advocate” according to the Myers-Briggs test. This would seem to line up with the
Big Five test as well as with my personal experience. I am a fairly recluse person, filled
with a lot of self-doubt, and always end up helping everyone else but myself. I
don’t make friends easily and don’t have many of them. But I like to believe that the
friends I do have are trustworthy and can be relied on. I find it normal to help people
around me. It is more than the right thing to do for me. It is the natural thing to do. I
feel in my element when helping others. I generally have a hard time with creativity,
art, and imagination. But when I am helping others with their creative endeavours I
suddenly burst with ideas. There is a quiet cynicism to me though. As I push everyone
up, it seems that I only fall down. It’s not just that I like helping people, it’s that I can
only help people and nothing else.


In a team, I might find it hard to initiate the conversation or lead the group if that is
necessary as I am generally introverted. I would not deal with team drama or hyper-
competitiveness very well as I am very agreeable and work better in a supportive
environment where team members regularly assist one another. I can let criticism get
to me and give up too easily. I tend to have high sudden bursts of energy where I study
attentively and enthusiastically but also sharp lows where I ignore work because I am
not in the right mood. Since I tend to help others more, I find that I have a difficult time
asking for help and focusing on my own work.


In a team, I should aim to start up discussions regularly and organise meetings more. I
should keep in contact with my colleagues and strive to put that helping nature to
good use. I should take and give criticism fairly and treat it as a group learning
experience to better the project. I should not let my assistance of other group
members over-shadow my responsibilities and work. I should take assistance when
needed and not just help others to my deterrent. I should take more advantage of my
sudden bursts of energy to get more work done while also making sure to keep a
steady flow of work going and not lose motivation.
